4-Stroke Engine Animation

This animation shows how intake, compression, power, and exhaust strokes work together inside a gasoline engine.

Intake Compression Power Exhaust

Components

  • Intake and exhaust valves control flow in and out of the cylinder.
  • Spark plug ignites the compressed air-fuel mixture.
  • Piston moves up and down from combustion force.
  • Connecting rod and crankshaft convert linear motion to rotation.
